Protocol ||Davidtun||

Protocol ||DAVIDTUN|| is an ASAS-issued directive classified under the highest order of operational secrecy. Its primary objective is to protect, destroy, and/or conceal extremely classified information from any exposure to the public, unauthorized personnel, or visitors.

The directive may be executed with the utmost severity—even at the cost of human lives—and, in the most extreme scenarios, the termination of the entire species may be deemed acceptable to uphold the integrity of the information.

Under a Level 5 Clearance, full control of ASAS will be temporarily transferred to ACG, which holds both the directive and authority to deploy the ||OMEGATUN WARHEAD||.

Additionally, under the directive of Protocol ||DAVIDTUN||, ASAS may initiate lockdown procedures on electronic devices located within or around the facilities of authorized employees. This includes systems suspected of containing redacted or sensitive data, especially during non-operational hours.
