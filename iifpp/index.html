IIFPP Knowledge Base

The IIFPP (Innovation Inc Fusion Power Plant) is an unofficial fan-made project as a tribute to Innovation Inc. Players influence the outcome by adjusting heat and cooling systems, impacting the reactor's stability.

Lore & History

Originally established by site director Yohn Macjames, the island was discovered in the 1950s. After his passing, it was acquired by ffyffhhalop.
