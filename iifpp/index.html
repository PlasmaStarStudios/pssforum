IIFPP

The IIFPP, also known as the Fusion Plant, is an unofficial fan-made game created by Plasmastar Studios as a tribute to Innovation Inc. Innovation Inc., at its core, revolves around creating and playing games centered on a reactor. Players influence the outcome by adjusting heat and cooling systems, which directly impact the reactor's temperature and stability.

LoreWise

The IIFPP is a nuclear power plant and research facility originally established by former site director Yohn Macjames. After his passing, it was acquired by ffyffhhalop. Located on a small island in international waters, the island was eventually incorporated into Generica as a subcontinent.

Project Information

Head project leader Ffyffhhalop2 originally started this project back in 2021. In 2023, it was officially released for beta testing. Over time, the game shifted to a free-to-play model, allowing a wider audience to access the facility without charge.
