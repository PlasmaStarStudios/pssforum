IIFPP Knowledge Base

The IIFPP (Innovation Inc Fusion Power Plant) serves as a primary research facility and nuclear power utility. As a tribute project to Innovation Inc., the facility utilizes advanced thermal exchange systems where personnel must actively manage cooling protocols to prevent catastrophic reactor failure.

Historical Archive

The facility was established on a remote island by Yohn Macjames in the 1950s. Following the director's passing, oversight was transferred to ffyffhhalop, leading to the current ASAS-Directive infrastructure.
